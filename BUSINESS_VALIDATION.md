# Sacred Life Studio
# Business Validation Report

Version 0.1

---

# Purpose

This document exists to answer one question:

**Is Sacred Life Studio worth spending the next 1–3 years building?**

The goal is not to justify the idea.

The goal is to challenge it.

Every assumption must be supported by evidence.

If evidence proves us wrong, we change direction.

---

# Problem Statement

Digital product creators spend an enormous amount of time switching between disconnected tools.

They research in one place.

Generate content somewhere else.

Manage projects elsewhere.

Track ideas in spreadsheets.

Analyze competitors manually.

Publish separately.

There is no single operating system for building and growing a digital product business.

---

# Hypothesis

Sacred Life Studio can become the operating system for AI-assisted digital product businesses.

Its purpose is not simply generating content.

Its purpose is helping creators make better business decisions while reducing the time required to execute them.

---

# Questions We Must Answer

Before investing significant development time we must answer:

• Is the market large enough?

• Who is the ideal customer?

• What problems are they willing to pay to solve?

• What software are they already paying for?

• What gaps exist today?

• What becomes our competitive advantage?

• What makes Sacred Life Studio difficult to replace?
