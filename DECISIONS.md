# Sacred Life Studio Decisions

This document records every major architectural, product, and business decision.

The purpose is to preserve context.

Months from now we should understand not only WHAT we decided, but WHY.

---

# 2026-07-02

## Decision

Sacred Life Studio will be built for personal daily use before commercial release.

### Reason

Software should solve real workflow problems before attempting to solve other people's.

If the founder naturally chooses Sacred Life Studio over existing tools every day, that is strong evidence of product value.

### Expected Outcome

A product designed from real experience instead of assumptions.

---

# 2026-07-02

## Decision

The platform will optimize for better business decisions rather than content generation.

### Reason

AI content generation is becoming widely available.

Decision support creates more durable value.

### Expected Outcome

Users spend less time creating products that fail.

---

# 2026-07-02

## Decision

The platform will remain AI-provider agnostic.

### Reason

Technology changes quickly.

The software should adapt without requiring a complete rewrite.

### Expected Outcome

OpenAI, Claude, Gemini, local models, and future providers can all become interchangeable components.

---

# 2026-07-02

## Decision

Human approval remains required for critical business and code decisions.

### Reason

AI should assist judgment, not replace it.

### Expected Outcome

Higher trust and fewer costly mistakes.

---
